Artist Statement


Infinite Worlds is an exploration of play and creativity through an infinite world generated by code.
This project utilizes Prof. Wes Modes framework to create synergy between art and code.
Infinite Worlds invites participants to question how randomness generates order in our natural
environment while inspiring playful experimentation.

Technical

I utilize a combination of procedural generation algorithms to create a dynamically evolving virtual landscape.
Building upon Prof. Modes initial framework, I uniquely created a generator that makes towers of dishes, in differing variety and number, as well as differing angles.
.

Reflection

This was challenging and exciting. I had a quirky idea and ran with it, and when thinking of things that were truly infinite in this world, my roomate's dishes came to mind. I struggled alot with the generator itself. I also struggled to find assets, so I made them all myself.
